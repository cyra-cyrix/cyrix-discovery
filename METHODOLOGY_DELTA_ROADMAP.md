## Delta milestones (replaces the old M2→M3 boundary; old M3–M7 fold into D6)

**D1 — The organizational model in the loop.** Org store (entities, workflows, org-level unknowns + contradiction registers) built OVER the M1 evidence envelopes; entity-resolution v1 (deterministic matching + consultant correction UI — human-in-loop per §21); every completed session updates the model (Principle 12). *This is the cross-interview brain's memory; pulls forward parts of old M3.* Effort: L.

**D2 — Question planner + questioncraft.** Candidate-question objects (§7: purpose, unknown_addressed, claim_being_tested, expected gain) generated from the org model at the **topic level**, entering the interview ONLY through the Runtime's §15 priors channel (topic priorities + neutral hypothesis topics) — the deterministic dispatcher is untouched, so no Runtime conflict. Consultant-grade topic seeds and per-move REALIZE questioncraft (Layers A–C structures). *This is where "brilliant questions" land.* Effort: L.

**D3 — Multi-session orchestration.** Consent state; Sessions 1/2/3 + micro-sessions with per-session objectives fed by the org model; invite lifecycle rework (multi-use, per-session); scheduling; §22 states. Durability rails reused wholesale. Effort: L.

**D4 — Cross-verification loop.** Layer D/E: contradiction-driven targeted follow-ups routed to the right roles; rule-based participant selection v1 (§8 factors, no ML); unknowns drive the next-session agenda. Effort: M–L (depends D1–D3).

**D5 — Privacy split.** Identity separated from content; pseudonymized findings; min-source thresholds; access logging; dashboard rework away from person-keyed exposure. **Product decisions required from owner on what the team may see.** Effort: L, parallelizable after D1.

**D6 — Findings → recommendations (old M3–M5 aligned to §10/§17/§23-with-bands).** Maturity L2–3 corroboration, consultant review workflow, recommendation objects, §24 quality tests as CI gates. Unblocks what was "Milestone 3." Effort: L.

**Sequencing rationale:** D1 before everything — smart questions are a function of organizational memory, which is the single missing organ. D2 delivers the felt quality improvement. D3/D4 make it an investigation rather than an interview. D5 before any real-participant rollout at scale. Old discipline holds: corpus-tested, invariant-gated, deployed and verified per milestone; runtime stays behind its flag; fallbacks never removed.
